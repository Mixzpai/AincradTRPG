STRUCTURE
---------
- 100-floor floating iron-and-stone castle, ~10km base diameter
- Each floor slightly smaller than the one below
- Each floor has cities, towns, villages, forests, plains, lakes
- One stairway per floor, beyond the Labyrinth boss room
- Quarter floors (25, 50, 75, 100) have the hardest bosses
- Multiples of 10 have second-hardest bosses
- Players ascend from Floor 1 (bottom) to Floor 100 (top)

SWORD SKILL MECHANICS
  - Activated by performing a "Pre-Motion" (e.g., raising sword above shoulder)
  - Weapon glows in skill-specific color when recognized
  - System auto-completes the attack at superhuman speed
  - Unlocked by raising weapon proficiency to certain levels
  - Higher proficiency = more skills + mod slots
  - Post-motion delay after skill completes (vulnerability window)

CRYSTAL ITEMS
-------------

  Teleport Crystal (blue)   — Instant warp to any previously visited town
  Healing Crystal (green)   — Instant full HP restore
  Antidote Crystal           — Cures status effects
  Corridor Crystal           — Opens portal for a group to a location
  Recording Crystal          — Stores images/voice messages
  Mirage Sphere              — 3D map display item

  All crystals are rare and expensive, used mainly in emergencies.
  Appearance: eight-sided colored pillars.

GAME MECHANICS
--------------

  - Death in-game = death in real life (NerveGear fries the brain)
  - Safe zones: towns and cities where PvP damage is nullified
  - Cursor colors: Green (normal), Orange (criminal), Red (murderer)
  - Immortal Object: indestructible game objects (buildings, NPCs in safe zones)
  - Col: in-game currency
  - Stats: STR (strength), AGI (agility), VIT (vitality) — no class system
  - Durability: equipment degrades with use, needs repair
  - Enhancement: upgrading equipment (+1, +2, etc.) with chance of failure
  - Last Attack bonus: player who lands killing blow gets best drops
  - Switching: party tactic — members alternate tanking during post-motion delays
  - No magic system — combat is entirely physical/skill-based
  - Party: up to 6 members; multiple parties form a raid group
  - Boss rooms: cannot leave once entered; crystals don't work inside
  - Teleport Gates: in every main town, must be activated by reaching floor first
  - Marriage system: shared inventory storage
  - Floor 1 took ~1 month to clear; later floors took days to weeks
  - ~10,000 players trapped, ~4,000 died, death game lasted ~2 years
  - Frontline clearing group was roughly 30-50 players at any time
